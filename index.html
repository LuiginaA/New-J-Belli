Meet Dr Janique

Your Dedicated Chiropractor

I earned my Master's Degree in Chiropractic from the University of Johannesburg, accredited by the European Council of Chiropractic. I am registered with the Allied Health Professions Council of South Africa (AHPCSA) and am a member of the Chiropractic Association of South Africa (CASA).

My approach combines advanced chiropractic techniques with genuine care for each patient. I specialize in pregnancy and pediatric care, and I'm passionate about helping families achieve optimal health naturally.
